Membership

Fablab Winam Kisumu have three categories of membership namely;

    ● Sungura
    ● Kifaru &
    ● Simba
,

SUNGURA

Under this category, Members expected to register here are juniours from age 5 - 17 years. This will be done in two categories as individuals or groups (clubs). These kids will be those interested in Designing, eletronics or generally making.

The individual kids will pay a modest monthly subscription while groups will pay annual fee

KIFARU

Under this category, Members are student majorly from Universities and Technical Schools around. GRaduates who want the intership experiences and any other from learning Institutions or have not taken more than one year out of college

Members of this class will be paying block subscription fees. THe blocks are Monthly, 3 Months, 6 Months or annual. In addition, you will be required to pay cost of value of material you use according BOM and the actual consumption

SIMBA

Here, the Entreprenuers, Jua Kali Artisans and Industrialists will be the members
